What we offer

Our Services

From day-to-day bookkeeping to resolving complex SARS disputes — a complete suite of compliance services for South African businesses.

Monthly Bookkeeping

Accurate, up-to-date recording of all financial transactions — keeping your books clean and audit-ready every month.

Payroll

Timely and compliant payroll processing — including PAYE calculations, IRP5 certificates, and SARS submissions.

Management Accounts

Monthly or quarterly management reports to give you real financial insight for confident business decisions.

VAT Returns

Accurate VAT201 submissions to SARS on time — including VAT reconciliations and input/output analysis.

Income Tax Returns

Individual and corporate income tax submissions — including provisional tax, annual returns, and refund tracking.

Company Registrations

Fast and compliant CIPC company registrations — from name reservation to your incorporation certificate.

CIPC Amendments

Director changes, address updates, name changes, and annual returns filed accurately with the CIPC.

Beneficial Ownership

Assist with CIPC's mandatory beneficial ownership declarations — ensuring your company stays compliant with the latest regulations.

Trust Registrations

Registration of inter vivos and testamentary trusts through the Master of the High Court — correctly drafted and filed.

UIF / COID

Registration and compliance for Unemployment Insurance Fund and Compensation for Occupational Injuries and Diseases.

SARS Disputes & Remissions

Handling of SARS objections, appeals, remission applications, and payment arrangements on your behalf.

SARS Disputes, Remissions & Arrangements

Full dispute resolution including debt compromises, deferred arrangements, and penalty waiver negotiations with SARS.
